About Our Toffee

As a trusted brand with European roots, Alpenliebe is known for its exceptionally smooth and creamy, melt-in-your-mouth qualities. Our toffees are crafted with the finest ingredients, bringing a perfect blend of rich caramel flavor and silky smooth texture.

Available in almost 30 countries throughout the world, Alpenliebe is the preferred candy in China, India and other European and Asian markets.
